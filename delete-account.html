Delete Your Account

DiggerSafe (Digger Gray / DiggerSafe App) allows you to request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to Request Deletion

1. Email support@diggersafe.com.au from the email address linked to your DiggerSafe account.

2. Use the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

3. Include the email address associated with your account so we can locate it.

What Gets Deleted

Once your request is verified, we will permanently delete:

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Inspection records, photos, and service logs associated with your account
  • Fleet and machine data linked to your account

Timeframe

We will process deletion requests within 30 days of verification.

Local Data

You can also remove locally stored data on your own device at any time via Settings → Delete All Data within the app. Note this only clears data stored on your device — to delete your account and cloud-synced data, please follow the email request process above.

Questions

Contact us at support@diggersafe.com.au with any questions about this process.
